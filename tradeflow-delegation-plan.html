The Message to the Team
Hey team, I want to be honest with you all. I've been carrying too much, and in doing so, I haven't been giving you the room to actually lead. That changes now. TradeFlow NG is growing beyond what one person can manage well. So I'm restructuring how we work — not by piling more on you, but by giving each of you a domain that's yours. Real ownership. Real decisions. Real impact. I'll be focusing entirely on two things: improving the platform and securing funding. Everything else — content, community, data coordination, video, growth, field work, social media — belongs to you. This isn't me checking out. It's me trusting you to run your part of this properly. I'll be sharing individual roles with each of you. I want you to read yours, think about it honestly, and tell me: does this fit? Is there anything you need to do it well? You're not just contributors. You're the team that makes this real. Let's build this properly, together. — Quadri
What's Missing
5 Critical Team Gaps

These are the holes that will limit growth if not filled. If you have any recommendation for us, kindly reach out to me directly.

01
A Technical Co-founder or Lead Developer
Currently, we have only one technical person on the team. That's a single point of failure for the entire platform. If the person occupied with other tasks, platform development stalls completely.
02
A Business Development / Partnerships Person
Nobody on the team is specifically tasked with closing deals — onboarding paying traders, negotiating with cooperatives, or landing institutional relationships. Emmanuel does community, but BD requires a different muscle.
03
A Formal Financial / Operations Manager
When grant funds arrive and brokerage revenue starts flowing, we need someone who can manage disbursements, track expenses, and ensure financial accountability. Right now, this falls back on the CTO by default.
04
A Legal / Compliance Advisor (Even Informal)
As we do brokerage, handle agent agreements, and apply for grants, we're operating in legal territory without coverage. Agent contracts, data usage rights, and commodity trading regulations can create real problems without guidance.
05
A Senior Advisor / Industry Mentor
We have domain specialists being onboarded (economist, agronomist), but we don't have a senior figure — someone who has moved commodities, run agri-businesses, or navigated Nigerian market infrastructure — giving us strategic guidance and opening doors.
