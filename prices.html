📋 About this data

All prices are reported by verified TradeFlow NG field agents who physically visit assigned markets each week. Prices reflect actual observed transaction values — not listed or posted prices.

  • Data is collected every week, typically between Tuesday and Thursday on major market days
  • Each submission is timestamped and includes the agent's GPS location
  • Outlier detection (Z-score) removes entries that deviate significantly from the weekly average
  • Where two agents report the same market, the average is used and confidence is marked as high
  • TradeFlow NG does not guarantee prices — these are intelligence estimates for planning purposes only

Turn this data into a trade decision

Price data alone is not enough. TradeFlow NG's optimizer combines these prices with forecasts and route data to recommend the most profitable trade this week.
