Your Weekly Tasks

Simple. Consistent. Impactful.

Every week, you complete four tasks. Most agents finish in under two hours total.

1

Visit your assigned markets

Go to the 1–2 markets you listed at registration — ideally on a major market day. Walk through the commodity sections, observe prices, ask sellers, note what you see.

~45 mins
2

Fill the KoboToolbox form

Open the form link on your phone. Select your name and market, enter prices for each commodity, rate availability and quality, note any disruptions. Submit before you leave the market.

~20 mins
3

Take one photo

A clear photo of the goods on display or a price board if visible. One photo per commodity is enough. Photograph any road damage or market disruption you observe too.

~5 mins
4

Confirm via WhatsApp

After submitting, send a quick message to your supervisor: "Done — [Market Name], [Date]." This confirms your submission is recorded and processed.

~2 mins
