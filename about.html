Our Story

Built from a real problem, by someone who watched it happen

TradeFlow NG started not as a business plan but as a question: why does my uncle keep coming home with less money than he should, when he knows more about agricultural trade than most people will ever learn? The answer was not his skill. It was the information gap — and we built a system to close it.

"Every week, my uncle drives hours to sell his maize — sometimes at a loss because prices dropped. I built TradeFlow NG so that never has to happen again. Not to him. Not to any trader in Nigeria."

QA
Quadri Adebisi
Co-Founder & CEO — TradeFlow NG
Our Mission

Make agricultural trade intelligence accessible to every Nigerian trader

Nigeria's agricultural sector is worth over ₦7.8 trillion and employs more than 35% of the country's workforce. Yet the people at the centre of it — the traders who move food from farms to markets — make billion-naira decisions every week with almost no reliable information.

We believe that a trader in Nasarawa deserves the same quality of market intelligence that a commodity trader in Lagos gets. TradeFlow NG exists to close that gap.

We are building the data infrastructure that Nigeria's agricultural trade has always needed — a real-time, AI-enhanced, field-validated price intelligence network that covers the country's most active north-south commodity corridors.

We are not a charity. We are a technology company building a sustainable business model — because sustainable businesses outlast donor cycles and create lasting change.

📊

Data honesty

We show confidence intervals. We flag uncertain forecasts. We never present a guess as a certainty. Traders make real financial decisions based on our outputs — that demands honesty about what we know and what we don't.

🤝

Community first

Our agents are not data entry workers — they are local experts. Our traders are not users — they are partners in building the system. Every feature we build exists to serve them, not the other way around.

⚙️

Technical rigour

We use real machine learning (Prophet), real optimisation (PuLP linear programming), and real infrastructure (PostgreSQL on cloud). Not dashboards dressed up as intelligence. Real systems that produce real outputs.

🌱

Long-term thinking

We are building something that compounds. More agents means better data. Better data means better forecasts. Better forecasts mean more traders. More traders means more revenue for better agents. This loop is the business.

Our Journey

From idea to live system

💡
2024 — The spark

Watching the problem firsthand

Observing an uncle who trades maize and cassava across northern Nigeria making consistently suboptimal routing decisions — not from lack of skill, but from lack of information. The question: what if the data he needed already existed and just needed to be collected and analysed?

🏗
Early 2025 — Foundation

Building the technical core

Designed the database schema (9 tables), built the cleaning pipeline with Z-score outlier detection, and trained the first Prophet forecasting models. Supervised by Dr. R. A. Adeyemi, Department of Statistics.

⚙️
Mid 2025 — Optimisation

The optimizer and dashboards go live

Built the PuLP linear programming optimization engine — the system's core intelligence. Deployed two Streamlit dashboards: an admin control centre and a mobile-first agent interface. First optimization run generated 15 route recommendations.

☁️
Early 2026 — Cloud deployment

Live on Streamlit Cloud and Supabase PostgreSQL

Migrated from SQLite to PostgreSQL on Supabase. Both dashboards deployed on Streamlit Cloud. Authentication system live. Agent onboarding underway. Multiple grant applications submitted.

🌾
Now — SIWES and first real data

Field placement at NCAM, Ilorin

6-month SIWES placement at the National Centre for Agricultural Mechanization in Kwara State. Piloting TradeFlow NG in the field, collecting real Kwara commodity prices, and building institutional relationships with agricultural bodies.

→
Next — First real trade cycle

Live agent data and first brokered deals

16 agents across 8 states submitting weekly prices. First real optimization cycles on live data. TradeFlow NG makes its first broker trades — validating the system with real capital at risk. First paying trader subscribers.
